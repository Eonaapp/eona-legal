Privacy Policy (For Mainland China)

Last Update: July 7th, 2025

Eona (“we”, “us”) values your privacy and handles your personal data in compliance with the Personal Information Protection Law (PIPL) of the People’s Republic of China.

  1. Information We Collect
    We may collect the following types of personal data:
    • Phone number (for registration or verification)
    • Reading preferences, questions, and app interactions
    • Subscription status and transaction history
    • Device identifiers, operating system, location (approximate)
  2. Purpose of Collection
    We collect your data for the following reasons:
    • To provide personalized readings
    • To manage subscriptions and access rights
    • To improve app functionality and user experience
    • To ensure security and compliance
  3. How We Store & Share Your Data
    Storage: Data is stored securely in overseas cloud platforms (e.g., Firebase, Adapty).
    Transfer: Your data may be transferred outside Mainland China, to the United States or other jurisdictions. We use encryption and anonymization where possible.
    By using the App, you explicitly consent to this cross-border data transfer.
  4. Third-Party Processing
    We use the following third-party service providers:
    • Firebase (Google LLC) – analytics & cloud database
    • Adapty – subscription and purchase management
    • OpenAI – AI-generated reading logic
    These services may process your data outside China. We require them to take appropriate security measures.
  5. Your Rights Under PIPL
    As a user located in China, you may:
    • Request access or correction of your data
    • Withdraw consent at any time
    • Request deletion of your personal information (unless legally required to retain)
    • File a complaint with data protection authorities
    Please email us at support@eona.app to exercise any of these rights.
  6. Sensitive Personal Information
    We do not intentionally collect any sensitive personal data (e.g., biometric info, religious beliefs).
  7. Children’s Privacy
    This App is not directed at individuals under 18 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors.
  8. Changes to Policy
    We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Material changes will be announced in the App interface. Continued use indicates acceptance.
  9. Contact Us
    For any questions or requests, please email: support@eona.app
